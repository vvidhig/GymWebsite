Workout Schedule - BRO SPLIT

A bro split is a popular gym workout routine where each training session focuses on a single muscle group or two closely related ones. Typically, the split covers five days, allowing each major muscle group (chest, back, legs, arms, shoulders) to be trained on separate days.

The bro split allows intense focus on each muscle group, promoting hypertrophy (muscle growth) through high-volume training.

Monday Pull Back and Bicep
Tuesday Push Chest, Tricep and Shoulders
Wednesday Leg Legs and Abs
Thursday Pull Back and Bicep
Friday Push Chest, Tricep and Shoulders
Saturday Leg Legs and Glutes
